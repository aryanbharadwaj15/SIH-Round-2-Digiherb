Quiz Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully before starting the quiz:

  • The quiz consists of 10 questions related to plant identification.
  • You have 2 minutes to complete the quiz.
  • For each correct answer, 1 point will be awarded.
  • Skipping a question will not affect your score but no points will be given.
  • Max score: 10 points.
  • If you get an answer wrong, 0 points will be added to your score.
  • Once the time is over or all questions are answered, you will see a detailed score report.
